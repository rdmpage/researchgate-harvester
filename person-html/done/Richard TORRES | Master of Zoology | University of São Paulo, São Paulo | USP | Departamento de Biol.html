An adult female of Phrynus barbadensis (Pocock, 1893) (Phrynidae) was observed in a locality of the Caribbean region of Colombia while it was predating on a newly molted female of the same species. Projects (3)
Trophic ecology of amphibians and reptiles of Sucre, Colombia
Project
Our goal is to describe the feeding patterns of amphibians and reptiles in the department of Sucre and also in other areas of the Colombian Caribbean Region. We are interested in the study of intrapopulational and interespecific dietary variation, as well as in understand the effects of biotic and abiotic factors on trophic resource use patterns in ecological communities.
