About Graham
Introduction
My main research interests are in the taxonomy and functional morphology of the Bivalvia with a current focus on the Thyasiroidea . I also have long standing interest in the Arcoidea and in deep-sea bivalves. I am dedicated to the production of taxonomic tools such as my books on the Red Sea and Arabian Sea bivalves but now through web tools see I was editor of Journal of Conchology until 2017. I am working on the Mollusca of Kuwait. I am supporting the development of bivalve taxonomy in India.
