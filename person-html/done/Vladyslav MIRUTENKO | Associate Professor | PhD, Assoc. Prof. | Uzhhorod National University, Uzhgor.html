Introduction
I currently work at the Department of Entomology and Biodiversity Conservation, Uzhhorod National University. My actual researches are in Entomology (coleopterology, systematics) and Nature conservation. Current project is 'Fauna and ecology of the Ukrainian Carpathians Coleoptera.'

Uzhhorod Entomological Readings
Project
"Uzhhorod Entomological Readings" is a traditional forum of entomologists of the Carpathian region. It's an annual scientific conference on problems of general and applied entomology and conservation of the Carpathian nature. The conference is provided by the Department of Entomology and Biodiversity Conservation of the Uzhhorod National University and by the Transcarpathian branch of the Ukrainian Entomological Society. "Uzhhorod Entomological Readings" is a meeting of entomologists of Central Europe which aim to consolidate scientific potential for conservation of the Carpathians.

Biodiversity of Ukraine
Project
The State Museum of Natural History, National Academy of Sciences of Ukraine (Lviv) is seeking to develop an information center of the biodiversity in our country http://dc.smnh.org/ that will facilitate taxonomical work, and will help disseminate the current knowledge of the Ukrainian biota to the general public at large. All the information (systematic classification, geographical distribution, registered material in the systematic collections of the Museums, literature data, human observation etc.), which is relevant to a taxon can be assigned to portal. The portal is expected to be of interest to researchers, hobbyists, professionals, naturalists, policy makers, activists, educators and students, as well as the curious citizen. The portal is work in progress.
