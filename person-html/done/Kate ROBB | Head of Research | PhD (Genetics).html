Introduction
I am the Founding Director and Head of Research at the Marine Mammal Foundation. My PhD research led to the formal description and naming of the Burrunan dolphin, Tursiops australis. I have instigated & supervised numerous projects covering robust population modelling, genetics & genomics, geospatial mapping of marine mammal, social structure & toxicology studies. I am an Honorary Fellow at Deakin & Monash University, Research Associate at Museum Victoria, and the current President of Australian Marine Science Association, Victorian branch.
