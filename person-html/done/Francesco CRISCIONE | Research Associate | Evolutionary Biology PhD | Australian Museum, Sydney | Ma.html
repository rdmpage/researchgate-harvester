Into the abyss: systematics and evolution of temperate Australian deep-sea 'turrid' gastropods
Project
Deep-sea gastropod biodiversity in Australia is largely unexplored. However, material collected by ongoing surveys now facilitates such work. Turrids, a hyper-diverse gastropod group, are abundant in this material and largely represented by undescribed species. Taxonomic study on Australian turrids is scarce, based on shell morphology and with emphasis on shallow tropical taxa. In collaboration with MNHN, we aim to describe 50+ new species of temperate Australian deep-water turrids, revising existing species, and contributing toward revisions of several genera and one family. Additionally, we will explore the evolutionary affinities of the Australian target group in a global context.
