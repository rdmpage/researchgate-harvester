About Jørgen
Introduction
I am in interested in higher-level relationships within Crustacea. I base much of my work on morphological studies of crustacean larvae and limb development. I have a particular interest in many smaller but phylogenetically important crustacean taxa such as Branchiopoda, Leptostraca, Thermosbaenacea, Mystacocarida, Remipedia, and many others.
