Introduction
Research interests: systematics, biogeography, phylogeography, and speciation of plants, particularly alpine plants in the Southern Hemisphere. Research goals: Integrate molecular data with morphology, cytology and other data to better understand the taxonomy and geographical and morphological patterns of species, especially New Zealand species radiations. Study organisms: Ourisia, Veronica, Plantago (Plantaginaceae) and Myosotis (Boraginaceae).

Ourisia cotapatensis (Scrophulariaceae s.l.), a new species from Bolivia
Article
Full-text available
  • Dec 2003
  • Heidi Meudt
  • Stephan Beck
A new, rare species of Ourisia from the department of La Paz in Bolivia is described based on three recent collections. The new species, Ourisia cotapatensis, is closely related to Ourisia pulchella from Bolivia and Peru and the other species of Ourisia from the central and northern Andes.
