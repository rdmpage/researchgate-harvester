Projects

Projects (4)
Monograph on the genus Allium in E Asia
Project
The goals of this project are: 1) to expand the current knowledge on general morphology, anatomy, microstructures, somatic chromosome numbers and distribution by including unstudied species and different accessions of already reported and studied taxa, and 2) to address taxonomic issues, clarify type identifications, and provide a clear taxonomic treatment with new descriptions and illustrations of the species. This research will provide sound foundation for a future global monograph and the systematic understanding of Allium.
View
Flora of Mongolia
Project
1. Conservation status of endemic and rare plants. 2. Contribution knowledge on the vascular plant of west, east, north and south part of Mongolia. 3. Taxonomic revision of selected genus such as Allium L., Primula L, and Viola etc. 4. Taxonomic revision of water plants. 5. Herbarium collection for the future a digital herbarium website. Our project is supported by a research project (Grant Number KNA 1-2-20, 14-4) of the Korean National Arboretum (KNA), South Korea.
