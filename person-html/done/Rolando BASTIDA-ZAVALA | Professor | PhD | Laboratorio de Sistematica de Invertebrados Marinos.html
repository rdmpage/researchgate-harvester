Introduction
I am interested primarly in the taxonomy of serpulids (Polychaeta: Serpulidae); additionally, I work about other polychaete families as polynoids, nereidids, sabellids, sabellariids, eunicids, chrysopetalids, ctenodrilids (now in Cirratulidae) and pisionid (now Sigalionidae). Also, in general, I am interested in marine invertebrate biodiversity, mostly from hard bottoms, mangroves and coral reefs. Since a decade ago I studying the aquatic invasive species, mainly from the Tropical Eastern Pacific.
