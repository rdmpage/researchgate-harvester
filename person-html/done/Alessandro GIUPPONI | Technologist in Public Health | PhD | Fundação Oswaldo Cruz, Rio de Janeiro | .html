Diversity and taxonomy of neotropical shorttailed whipscorpions (Arachnida: Schizomida: Hubbardiidae)
Project
Little is known about the phylogenetic relationships between neotropical genera of Hubbardiidae. In this project, the goal is to increase the knowledge about the morphology of schizomids, to propose hypotheses of homologies between their morphological structures with taxonomic interest and the description of new neotropical taxa of this group, as well as to make known the real geographic distribution of the South American species. All these, necessary elements for the construction of a proposal of phylogenetic relations.
